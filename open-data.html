Open data

How do data March? In formation.

Are you looking for addresses for your marketing campaign or to get an ideas where to setup your business in York Region to cater for the most compatible audience?

York Region, Toronto, Mississauga and number of municipalities around are collecting and maintaining open and free datasets which you can download and use in any way you like.

You can get a list of local business parks, residential addresses, census details as well as fairly recent business directory. You can also find data on public transportation, roads and traffic lights, government finances and so on.

This is a very useful resource, formerly available only in the form of commercial publications (phone book anyone?). The more we used it and ask for, the more incentive will be for government to refine and improve it.
