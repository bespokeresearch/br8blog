Passwords Everywhere

Bespoke Research | Thu 19 December 2019

So many passwords, so little time...

How many times were you sitting at your desk and trying to remember what was your account password? Or writing your pin on a sticky and attaching it to your wallet?

Simple and free password manager can make your life so much easier. You only need to remember one password and the reset are generated and stored securely in the database.

First of all you can keep your passwords in your browser. All modern browser can store them in password protected storage. As you open the website, it offers you the right password, all you need to do is hit enter and login.

If you want to keep your passwords outside of your browser and for example transfer them between devices, give a try KeePass family of password managers, which are free and easy to use. Not only it can save them, it can also store other sensitive information as well as generate secure passwords for you so you don't have to spend time when you registering new account for yourself.
